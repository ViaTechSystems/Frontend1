Directions

The goal is to turn the select options into a well styled drag-and-drop interface. Any number of "Not Assigned Options" should be able to be selected and then dragged over to the "Assigned Options". Once options are dragged over, they should continue to be selected.

The height of the boxes holding the options should be responsive and fill the entire height of the remaining visible screen.
